Tips and Tricks

    Eat a wide variety of fresh fruits and vegetables by including different types and colours every day - they are low in calories but high in many essential nutrients like fibre, which can keep you feeling full for longer periods of time and aid in weight loss. Consume complex carbohydrates - they are found in vegetables, fruits, and whole grains, which are also good sources of vitamins, minerals, and fibre. Include whole grains, legumes or beans, nuts and seeds in your diet. Opt for lean meats and poultry, fish, eggs, which contain a good amount of protein that reduces your appetite and boosts metabolism. Choose mostly low-fat dairy products, such as milk, yoghurt, cheese and/or their alternatives (but reduced-fat milk is not suitable for children under 2 years)
